Welcome to our new website. We encourage you to find out more about the values and vision that guides us, the services and programs we offer, the indicators that ensure our continued accountability and transparency, and the news and events that matter most to you. This Hospital has a proud tradition of care. For Last so many years, this Hospital has cared for the people, surrounding communities, and beyond, being a significant player within the District. Residing in the heart of a beautiful cottage country on the shores of the Gomti River, we offer healthcare not only to our community residents but to the many visitors who frequent our beautiful area year round.
